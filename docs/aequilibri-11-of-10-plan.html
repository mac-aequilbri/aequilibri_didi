The 11/10 Plan

Today the product is a 7/10: exceptional architecture and a genuinely differentiated AI write-loop, held back by polish and production-readiness. This is the path from 7 → 10 → 11.

7 → 10 · close every gap

Earn the score you've already engineered

Your backend is at ~8, your surfaces trail it. Reaching 10 is almost entirely execution, not invention: green the baseline, make the UI as considered as the code, harden the integrations you've already stubbed.

10 → 11 · the bet

Make the AI-ops loop undeniable

11/10 isn't more features — it's focus. Resist breadth. Turn the approve-and-learn loop from a feature into the product's gravity: proactive, explainable, and visibly compounding. That's the thing no competitor has.

The plan

Five phases. The first three reach 10; the fourth is the 11/10 bet; the fifth makes it sellable. Sizing: S days · M weeks · L multi-week.

The 11/10 bets

Lean all the way into the moat. These are what make a buyer say "I've never seen that."

Moat 8 → 11

Proactive assistant

Flip from reactive chat to a scheduled agent (you already run an hourly cron). It watches the data, drafts proposals, and fills the approval inbox before you ask.

"Didi spotted 3 risks trending and drafted mitigations for your approval."

Explainable AI / trust layer

Every proposal shows its reasoning, the exact data it read, and which learning rules shaped it. Trust is the bottleneck for AI-in-operations — make it visible.

"Why did the AI suggest this?" — answered, every time.

Compounding learning, made visible

Turn the invisible learning loop into a headline metric: estimate accuracy and overrun reduction over time, per org. This is your renewal and sales story.

"Estimate accuracy +18% since adoption."

Cross-project intelligence

Portfolio-level insight: benchmark a new estimate against this org's own historicals, surface patterns across jobs. The platform gets smarter the more it holds.

Each project makes the next one sharper.
